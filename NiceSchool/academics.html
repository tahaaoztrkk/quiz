Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering spans solid mechanics, thermofluids, materials, manufacturing, and control. Learn CAD/CAE, FEA/CFD, and rapid prototyping while tackling projects in robotics, energy systems, and smart manufacturing. Internships and labs translate analysis into practical, reliable designs.

Neuroscience

Aeronautical Engineering

The Aeronautical Engineering program covers aerodynamics, flight mechanics, propulsion, structures, and avionics, blending theory with wind-tunnel testing and simulation. Build skills in CFD, FEM, and systems integration while working on UAVs, rockets, and aircraft design studios. Capstone projects and industry partnerships prepare you for aerospace R&D and certification environments.

  • 4 years
  • B.Sc. Degree
  • Fall & Spring

Data Science

The Data Science program blends statistics, machine learning, and scalable data engineering to transform raw information into actionable insight. Build end-to-end skills in Python/R, SQL, visualization, ML modeling, MLOps, and cloud platforms—applying them to real datasets from business, health, and the public sector with a strong focus on ethics and responsible AI.

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering program builds solid foundations in circuits, signals, electromagnetics, control, power systems, communications, and embedded hardware. Gain hands-on experience with FPGA/MCU platforms, PCB design, power electronics, and IoT—using tools like MATLAB, Python, SPICE, and CAD. Specialize in areas such as renewable energy, robotics, VLSI, or wireless systems through labs, capstone projects, and industry collaborations.
